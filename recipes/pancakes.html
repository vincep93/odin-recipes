Pancakes

Yum Yum nothing like some fluffy pancakes for breakfast.

What you will need:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Bisquick

    • Sounds easy enough! Heres how it will go together

      Put 2 cups of Bisquick in a bowl
      Put one cup of milk in the bowl
      Crack an egg into the bowl
      Pour 1/4s cup of oil in the bowl

    Now mix it all up until there are no clumps of dried batter. Heat up a skillet and butter her up! Once shes hot use the 1/4 cup that is already dirty to scoop out one pancake worth of mix and pour on the skillet!
